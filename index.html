About

I was previously a master's student in Data Science at Harvard, where I was advised by Dr. Weiwei Pan and Prof. Finale Doshi-Velez. I earned my B.S from University of California, Los Angeles, with a double major in Applied Mathematics and Statistics with a Specialization in Computing. I'm intersted in trustworthy and explainable AI, as well as the use of generative models in content creation. I look to leverage interpretability, generative models, and their intersection to drive positive social impact.
